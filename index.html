FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need a new phone or number?

No. Saarthi works with your existing WhatsApp. On Premium you can get a dedicated business number if you want one.

How long does setup take?

About 15 minutes — and we do it for you. Share your menu/services, prices and hours, and you're live the same day.

Will it sound like a robot?

No. It replies warmly and naturally in your customer's language, and only uses your real information — it never makes things up.

What if a customer asks something it doesn't know?

It politely directs them to call or message you directly. You stay in control of anything sensitive.

Can it really take full orders?

Yes (Growth & Premium). It collects name, items, quantity and address, confirms the order, pings you on WhatsApp, and logs it to a Google Sheet.

What does it cost to try?

Nothing — 7-day free trial, free setup, no credit card. Keep it only if it earns its keep.
